Why Organ Donation?

Organ donation is a generous and worthwhile decision that can save multiple lives. By donating your organs after you pass away, you can help patients in desperate need of transplants, giving them a new lease on life. It's a legacy of compassion and care that will live on in the lives of others.

Benefits of Organ Donation

  • Saves lives of those with organ failure.
  • Improves the quality of life for recipients.
  • Provides solace to donor families knowing their loved one helped others.
  • Fosters a sense of community and humanity.

Organ Donation Statistics

Did you know?

  • One donor can save up to 8 lives through organ donation.
  • Every 10 minutes, someone is added to the transplant waiting list.
  • Over 100,000 people are waiting for a life-saving transplant in the United States alone.
  • Sadly, 20 people die each day waiting for an organ that never comes.
